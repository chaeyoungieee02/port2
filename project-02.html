Rotoscoping Procedure

1

Import and prepare footage

Match the composition settings to the source clip and organize the workspace.

2

Set up the mask or roto tool

Create the initial shape or mask that defines the subject you want to isolate.

3

Create the first frame

Trace the subject on the first usable frame so the keyframes have a clear starting point.

4

Track frame by frame

Move through the footage and adjust the mask as the subject moves across the frame.

5

Refine edges and difficult motion

Use feathering, extra points, and careful correction around limbs, turns, and overlapping shapes.

6

Review and smooth the animation

Check for jitter and make the movement consistent before exporting the sequence.

7

Composite the final result

Add text, effects, or background changes once the roto layer is clean.

8

Export the finished video

Render the final output in the format required for presentation or submission.
